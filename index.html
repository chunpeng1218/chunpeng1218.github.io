Chun Peng

 

Hi, I am an assiciate professor in the Department of Logisitics Management in the School of Economics and Management at Beijing Jiaotong University in China. Before I was working as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal (the business school of Université de Montréal) and GERAD, where I was working with Professor Erick Delage. Prior to this, I obtained my Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering at Beijing Institute of Technology. During my Ph.D., I visited the Department of Decision Sciences of HEC Montréal from Oct. 2016 to Nov. 2017.

Research Interests: Data-driven optimization under uncertainty using stochastic/robust optimization and machine learning, and with applications in (a) sustainable urban transportation and logistics management (e.g., emergency medical service location, humanitarian logistics, last-mile delivery, shared mobility); (b) healthcare operations management (e.g., operating rooms planning, telemedicine scheduling); (c) Supply chain management (e.g., Live streaming in E-commerce supply chain).
